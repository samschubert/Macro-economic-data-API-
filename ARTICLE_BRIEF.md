## WHY COPPER/GOLD RATIO WORKS

### Supply-Side Neutralization
- Both metals often mined together
- Similar cost structures
- Supply shocks affect both similarly
- **Result**: Ratio isolates DEMAND dynamics

### Demand Signals
- **Copper**: Industrial demand, economic activity, manufacturing
- **Gold**: Safe haven, easing hedge, liquidity stress indicator
- **Ratio**: Pure signal of industrial optimism vs defensive positioning

### Strip Out the Noise
- Not affected by single-commodity supply disruptions
- Chilean copper strike? Affects both sides
- Gold mine closures? Affects both sides
- **Clean signal**: When copper outperforms = risk-on
