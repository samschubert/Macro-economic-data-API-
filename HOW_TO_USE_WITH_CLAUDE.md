## Troubleshooting

**Claude can't see the charts clearly?**

- Upload macro_data_export.csv
- Ask Claude to calculate specific correlations

**Want different tone?**
- Add to prompt: "More casual" or "More academic" or "More aggressive"

**Thread too long?**
- Ask: "Condense to 10 tweets, keep the killer stats"
